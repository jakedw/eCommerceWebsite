Bo Diddley's "Twang Machine" - $31,000

Item ID: 0009

Frequently referred to as "that rectangular guitar", Bo Diddley's "Twang Machine" is the one most closely associated with the very early days of rock music. The greatly influential blues / rock musician first created the rectangular-shaped guitar with Gretsch in 1958. He drew his inspiration from old homemade instruments by folk musicians, who made guitars out of cigar boxes. According to Diddley himself, the necessity for an instrument with a smaller body came after an incident with a Gibson L5, where he jumped around the stage a bit too much and eventually hit his groin with the guitar.
